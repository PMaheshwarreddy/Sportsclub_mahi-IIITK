Volley ball

Trainings take place on monday and thursday 15.00 at Volley Ball courts Location: Volley Ball Court, IIITK Outdoor Stadium All the students who are intrested in the volleyball, can register for it. IIITK volleyball training is divided into parts: - The warming part is focused on ensuring that all muscles are trained to prevent injuries; - In the main part, technical skills are improved through both volleyball exercises and physical exercises with or without additional equipment; - In the final part of the course, students are given the opportunity to use all the exercises in the game, as well as to carry out rehearsal exercises.

Basket ball

Basketball trainings take place in two trainings a week, where one part is dedicated to the improvement of individual technique, while the other half is dedicated to active play. If the number of visitors in a training is smaller, the coaches pay more individual attention, which will definitely give additional satisfaction to the student for learning and improving new skills. Men's basketball, as it is known, at IIITK is highly developed and teams in these sports start in the leading championships, so this is the right time to remind that these training sessions do not take place with professional athletes, so everyone can feel comfortable and meet players of a similar level if the sport is a close hobby. Location: IIITK basketball court. All students can participate in the trainings, regardless of the level of mastery. The workout is divided into parts: 1. Warm-up part, which pays attention to basketball technique (drift, posture, litter, etc.) 2. The next stage is basketball skills development exercises (quick attacks, throwing competitions, etc.) 3. In the final part of the training, students have the opportunity to use everything they have learned in a basketball game (2 against 2, 3 against 3, 4 against 4 or 5 against 5).
